Critical System

Creating an eCommerce website is a critical step in assisting businesses in spreading their brands and increasing their ability to reach potential customers on the Internet, thereby increasing revenue and profit. The system is forecasted to increase profitability by 50% and if it does not run in an optimal state, it can create frustration among potential buyers, which can take a toll on brand trust. Buyers are also more likely to purchase from different competitors with similar product ranges as the market is polyamory.

System Downtime

Evidently, it's ideal for the organisation to have zero downtimes, however, it is difficult to achieve. Hence, the acceptable downtime must be calculated. You have to take into account the current exposure (the issue which causes downtimes in the first place, i.e., security risks, scheduled maintenance etc.) as well as available budget to determine how long can the downtime last.

BUSINESS CONTIGENCY PLAN


The Definition: A business contingency plan is a strategy for how your organization will respond to important or business-critical events that knock your original plans off track. Executed correctly, a business contingency plan can mitigate risk and help you get back to business as usual—as quickly as possible.


The Examples:

  • Environmental contigency plan: Governments and businesses in regions prone to earthquakes create preparedness initiatives and contingency plans.
  • Technology contigency plan: If your business is particularly data-heavy, for example, ensuring the safety and security of your information systems is critical.

PASSWORD SECURITY & PROTECTION

Password security and password protection are practices for establishing and verifying identity and restricting access to devices, files, and accounts. They help ensure that only those who can provide a correct password in response to a prompt are given access.

Embed security in training and culture

Deploy multi-factor authentication

Protection from cyberattacks is only as strong as the weakest link. It's important that users understand the impact of their password security practices.

Change passwords frequently

Create long, complex passwords

Passwords should be at least 10 characters in length. They should also contain a combination of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Safely store passwords as credentials

Use biometric passwords (physical features)

Biometric passwords provide physical proof of identities using devices that scan attributes such as fingerprints, faces, and voices to improve credibility.
